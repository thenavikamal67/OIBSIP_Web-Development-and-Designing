About Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was one of India's most respected scientists and served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. He played a vital role in India's missile and space programs, earning the title "Missile Man of India". He believed that education, hard work, and determination could transform the lives of young people. His inspiring speeches and books continue to motivate millions across the world. Despite holding the highest office in the country, he remained humble, simple, and deeply connected with students. Dr. Kalam's life teaches us to dream big, work hard, and serve the nation with dedication. His legacy continues to inspire generations.
